Introducing "Alta Medical Transportation," where compassionate care and reliable transport intertwine to ensure your well-being is our top priority. With a steadfast commitment to excellence, we redefine medical transportation by delivering seamless, safe, and comfortable journeys for all.

Non-Emergency Medical Transportation

We provide non-emergency transportation services for patients who require transportation to and from medical appointments, outpatient procedures, rehabilitation sessions, or any other non-emergency medical visits.

Wheelchair Transportation

Our specialized vehicles are equipped to safely transport patients who rely on wheelchairs for mobility. Our trained staff ensures secure and comfortable transfers to and from the vehicle, prioritizing the individual's safety and well-being.

Insurance Coordination

We assist patients in navigating insurance requirements and coordinating transportation coverage, making the process seamless and stress-free.
